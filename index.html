About Me


As a designer with both front end experience and a business background, I have the ability to turn concepts to reality. My focus is in web based and native mobile applications because of the integral role that they can play in someone’s daily life.

Since the first project I was a part of (HealthyOut) was inducted into TechStars, I have pursued Product Design to help people get things done by improving the digital experiences in their life. For me the design process is the route to the solution, not just a deliverable product. I take a lean approach to get to the right answers through guerrilla usability testing of multiple concepts. Being able to move quickly with a small team, prototype and build out these concepts allows me to gather real feedback beyond clickable image mocks.
