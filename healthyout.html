HealthyOut

How a concept on a napkin made it to one of the
top 10 iOS health apps.

We designed HealthyOut because finding healthy food amongst an endless list of take-out options is far from easy. With a mobile-only approach, we designed around pre-existing takeout behaviors and modified them to eliminate junk food.

From Concept to Top 10

Working closely with the co-founder of HealthyOut, @wdot, we took HealthyOut from a concept to one of the top 10 food apps on the AppStore.

Onboarding Flow

Joining HealthyOut was also about pre-defining your dietary restrictions. By selecting a pre-made set dietary preferences, you could avoid any meals outside of your dietary restrictions.

Search Flow

Search initially presented our biggest challenge because it is a combination of taste, pre-set dietary restrictions and additional search criteria which made it difficult. Letting the user filter by restaurant, meal and by map locations makes it easier to find what you are looking for.
