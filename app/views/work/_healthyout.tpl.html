Working closely with the co-founder of HealthyOut, @wdot, we took HealthyOut from a concept to one of the top 10 food apps on the AppStore.

Designed for the problem at hand

We designed HealthyOut because finiding healthy food amongst an endless list of take-out options is far from easy. With a mobile only approach, we designed around pre-existing takeout behaviors and modified them to eliminate junk food.

Onboarding Flow

Joining HealthyOut was also about pre-defining your dietary restrictions. By selectig a pre-made set dietary preferences, you could avoid any meals outside of your dietary restrictions.

Search Flow

Search initally presented our biggest challenge because it is a combination of taste, pre-set dietary restrictions and additional search criteria which made it difficult. Ensuring that it was an iterative process that could be filtered by resturaunt, meal and by map locations makes it easier to find what you are looking for.
