Working closely with the co-founder of HealthyOut, @wdot, we took HealthyOut from a concept to one of the top 10 food apps on the AppStore.

Good ideas start from real problems

By studying patterns already developed around takeout services like Seamless, GrubHub and vendors like Jenny Craig gave us a better definition of success: a service that allows you to find meals for dining in or takeout that doesn’t force you to change your habits.

Gathering reactions, not opinions

Turning wireframes to prototypes and showing them to potential customers resulted in identifying actual problems in the design early on before we ever hired developers.
