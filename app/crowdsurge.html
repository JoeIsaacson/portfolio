Ticket Platform Redesign

By creating an agile design process that allowed us to rapidly build and test prototypes, we made it easier for our clients fans to buy tickets.

Revamped e-commerce experience

As the leader of the re-design of our global ticketing platform, we started from the beginning of supporting our core online business and then expanded into the physical with a complete, end to end POS system for people buying tickets in person.

Simplified Ticket Select

By breaking down the ticket select process into a few key steps, we have made the process a more conversational flow that continues to support GA and Reserved Tickets.

Select desired quantity

Select desired seats

Successful ticket return

Streamlined Registration

With 83% of ticket sales coming from new customers, we streamlined our registration process for new users and make it easier for existing customers to login.

New customer sign up

Authentic Brand Integraitons

As a white label product that is customized by our clients (ranging from Paul McCartney to Kendrick Lamar) it was important to make sure that the visual design followed the hierrarchy of the most important design elements.


By testing custom implementations of the platform I created a set of rules and best practices that allowed our visual designers to easily balance the clients brand design and the design of the application.
